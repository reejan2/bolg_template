Orchid School, awarded as One of the Best Higher Secondary Schools (10 +2) in the nation,
has been established as a brand in the realm of +2 education in Nepal.

Since its inception in 1997, Orchid has sincerely been nurturing teenage minds: strengthening
and empowering their potential and aspirations. As a result, hundreds of successful graduates
have been produced, many of whom are now into reputed professional careers. Through its
curricular and extra-curricular activities, Orchid consciously stimulates 21st-century skills:
Critical thinking, Communication, Collaboration, and Creativity.

Orchid amalgamates a galaxy of highly reputed professors, vastly experienced lecturers,
24×7 dedicated mentors, and never-tiring admin executives who prepare students not
only for NEB finals but also for competitive bachelor entrance, Careers, and life.
Through the regular lectures, entrance preparatory workshops/ tests, extra- extracurricular
activities, the orchid faculty members and executives impart quality knowledge, and execute
practical sessions for exposure and future Careers.

The ECA department offers students both national and international platforms to showcase
their talents and arts. The counselling and care department looks after each individual
focusing on his/her aspirations as. family background, career prospects, etc.

To conclude, Orchid is a home beyond your sweet home. You feel proud to be an Orchidian
and Orchid alumnus. At Orchid, you don’t simply get the syllabic knowledge; but every single day,
you are inspired and encouraged to reinvent yourself as somebody that the nation canbe proud.
